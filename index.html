St. John’s Roman Catholic Church:

Is a welcoming and responsive bilingual faith community, nourished by the Word of God and the Eucharistic Table. We are open and trusting in the love of Jesus Christ to advance the power of the Holy Spirit, within the parish and beyond, by living the Gospel. Our Church is composed of two Parish communities: St. John the Baptist and Our Lady of Guadalupe. Our Masses, events and services are provided both in English and Spanish. St. John’s community provides its parishioners with many opportunities for a vibrant sacramental life while celebrating a rich multicultural heritage. Welcome to our community!

Father Francisco Cruz

Secretary Glory Montoya
